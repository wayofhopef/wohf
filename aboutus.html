About Us!

We help people those who are suffering from the disease of Addiction, Such as: Alcohol, Drugs, weed and other subtance abuses and behaviour problems. we teach how to survive life without the use of Alcohol and Drugs. In "Way of Hope Foundation" you will get friendly and familyer environt and experience staffs (Recovering Addicts), Residential Doctor and Nurse for detoxification period, counsellor with experience, yoga and Meditation, valuable Sessions, 24 x 7 observation, indoor games like Carrom, Ludo and Chress, half way home practice/ follow-up facilities.

Our Mission

Our Mission is to help every addicts & alcoholics to live a happy & clean life whoever comes in contact with us for seeking recovery.

Our Vission

Our Vision is to be the best treatment center in giving recovery to addicts & alcoholics in a safe friendly atmosphere.

Our Value

Recovery Centered Care Patient & Family Oriented Professional Treatment.

Why Choose Way Of Hope Foundation?

Rehabilitation is care that can help you get back, keep, or improve abilities that you need for daily life. These abilities may be physical, mental, and/or cognitive (thinking and learning). You may have lost them because of a disease or injury, or as a side effect from a medical treatment. Rehabilitation can improve your daily life and functioning.

Our list of treatments include, detoxification, counseling, meditation, therapy sessions for better progress. The program is essentially voluntary with absolutely no use of Psychiatric medications unless there is an underlying disorder that surfaces during or after detoxification. We aim at treating our clients in the best possible manner and help them in a way that makes their life better and happier.
